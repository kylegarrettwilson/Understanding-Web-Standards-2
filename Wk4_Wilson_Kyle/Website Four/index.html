Welcome to SMILE BLACK. My name is Kyle Wilson and SMILE BLACK is a project of musical and visual expression through the eyes of an self proclaimed audiophile. I have a weekly upload schedule which consists of original pieces, various covers, and a vlogging series. My hopes are to facilitate an atmosphere where I can display the music that moves me as well as discuss the current trends in the industry. I hope you enjoy. Subscribe if you do and subscribe if you don't.

Upload Schedule

The Original Series is where all of my musical creations will live. I encourage any artist to use this content for their medium in whatever way possible. If you want to collaborate with an original idea, please contact me and we will set something up. You can contact me by the form below or messaging me on YouTube or Facebook.

The Cover Series is the roots of the SMILE BLACK project. I began recording drum covers four years ago and have loved every minute. It forces me to continue progressing and developing my skill as a well rounded musician. If you have any suggestions for future covers please message me below. Also, hit up the YouTube comments and my Facebook page.

The Vlogging Series is a community project that hopes to discuss the current trends throughout music and the industry surrounding it. The current state of the music industry is questionable and I intend for the SMILE BLACK community to be a beacon of honesty in a very commercialized business. Please feel free to make response videos and message them to me (I will feature you). Also, hit up the YouTube comments and my Facebook page.
